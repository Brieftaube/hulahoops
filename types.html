Dance Hoops
Typical hula hoops for people who are passionate about hooping tricks and want to enhance their skills by purchuasing a little bit more proffesional hula hoop. Materials used here vary, from plastic to polypro, design could be anything you can imagine. There is even speciall, folding, traveller-friendly and of course expensive, type of hula hoop. All you need for daily practice hides behind the term "dance hoop".

Fitness Hoops
People whose aim is to loose some weight by practice with a hula hoop should look around for fitness hoops. They usually have special tabs designed to stimulate body's muscles, are heavier and bigger than dance hoops. Not perfect for trying new tricks, they are the best for working out with a hula hoop.

Fire Hoops
Dream of every advanced and risk-seeking hula hhoper, fire hoop. In fact, it is one of the safiest equipment which street performances used to set on fire. It is not exactly hula hoop which is burning around your body, but kevlar, fireproof material, which is pinned to protrunding sticks. What is expecially nice is a crowd reaction exposed to a view of a fire hula hooper. No matter how confident you are with your hoop, they will be amazed. Unless you set yourself in fire...

Glow Hoops
For ones who are looking for less dangerous toy to play with, glow hoops are excellent, but pretty expensive choice. Being able to amaze people without taking a risk of burning yourself by an accident is interesting opportunity. Glow Hoops are made with adoption of LED technique.
