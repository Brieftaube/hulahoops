TYPES OF HULA HOOPS

Dance Hoops

Typical hula hoops for people who are passionate about hooping tricks and want to enhance their skills by purchuasing a slightly more professional hula hoop. Materials used here vary from plastic to polypro and the design could be anything you can imagine. There is even a special, folding, traveller-friendly and of course expensive, type of hula hoop. All you need for your daily practice hides behind the term "dance hoop".

Fitness Hoops

People whose aim is to lose some weight by practicing with a hula hoop should look for fitness hoops. They usually have special tabs designed to stimulate body's muscles, are heavier and bigger than dance hoops. Not perfect for trying new tricks, they are the best for working out with a hula hoop.

Fire Hoops

The dream of every advanced and risk-seeking hula hooper: the fire hoop. In fact, it is one of the safest equipments which street performances use to set on fire. It is not exactly a hula hoop which is burning around your body, but kevlar, fireproof material, which is pinned to protrunding sticks. What is expecially nice is the crowds reaction exposed to seeing a fire hula hooper. No matter how confident you are with your hoop, they will be amazed. Unless you set yourself on fire...

Glow Hoops

For the ones that are looking for a less dangerous toy to play with, glow hoops are an excellent, but pretty expensive choice. Being able to amaze people without taking the risk of burning yourself by an accident is an interesting opportunity. Glow Hoops are made with adoption of LED technique.
